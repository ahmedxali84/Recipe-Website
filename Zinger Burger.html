What is zinger Burger

The zinger is to denote the spicy hot flavor of the breading. This is probably the result of a year long effort by dozens of highly paid sales and marketing executives. After a year someone said “so what do we do boss?” The boss says, “sprinkle some cayenne on it and call it a zinger burger.”

Why is Zinger Burger So Good

The jalapenos made it a lot better and the patty was crunchy. The sauce was tangy, which combined with the salad and the patty, made a combination worth spending your money on. Which Country Invented Zinger Burger

It has enjoyed popularity across the globe since its inception in Trinidad and Tobago in 1984. More Zingers are sold in the UK, India and Australia than anywhere else. Australia alone sells more than 22 million Zingers each year.

INGREDIENTS

  • Zinger Fillets Boneless Chicken Breast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Corn starch
  • Monosodium Glutamate (MSG)
  • Bread Crumbs

Instructions:

For the Chicken Fillet:

  1. Marinate The Chicken:
    • In a bowl, mix the buttermilk, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
    • Add the chicken breasts to the marinade, ensuring they are fully coated.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  2. Flatten The Chicken:
    • Remove Chicken From The Marinate
    • Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound with a meat mallet until they are about 1/2 inch thick.
    3. Breeding And Frying:
    • In a large bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and baking powder.
  4. Bread The Chicken:
    • Dredge each chicken fillet in the flour mixture, ensuring they are fully coated. Press the flour onto the chicken to ensure it sticks well.
  5. Fry The Chicken:
    • Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 350°F (175°C).
    • Fry the breaded chicken fillets in batches until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
    • Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  6. Make the Souce:
    • In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
  7. Assemble The Burger
    • Toast the burger buns lightly.
    • Spread a generous amount of sauce on the bottom half of each bun.
    • Place a fried chicken fillet on top.
    • Add lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, and pickles.
    • Top with cheese if desired.
    • Cover with the top half of the bun.
  8. Serve Immediately:
    • Serve the Zinger Burger immediately while the chicken is still crispy and hot.
Enjoy your homemade Zinger Burger!
